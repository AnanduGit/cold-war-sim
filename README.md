# Cold War Simulator

## Features

### Gameplay Mechanics
- **Dynamic Events**: Players will experience random events that can alter the course of the game, simulating the unpredictability of the Cold War era.
- **Resource Management**: Players must manage military, economic, and diplomatic resources effectively to maintain their position on the global stage. 

### 3D Visualization
- **Immersive Environments**: Detailed 3D landscapes representing various Cold War hotspots, complete with authentic buildings and terrains.
- **Realistic Unit Models**: 3D models of military units and equipment that are historically accurate and well-rendered.

### Controls
- **User-friendly Interface**: Intuitive controls that allow players to easily navigate the game.
- **Customizable Control Layout**: Players can adjust controls to fit their preferred style, enhancing accessibility.

### Combat Systems
- **Turn-based Strategy**: Players engage in turn-based combat, allowing for strategic planning and tactical execution.
- **Unit Types**: Variety of units including infantry, armor, and air support, each with unique strengths and weaknesses.

### Technical Details
- **Engine**: Built using Unreal Engine, providing high-quality graphics and smooth gameplay.
- **Supported Platforms**: Available on Windows, macOS, and Linux, ensuring a wide reach for players.
- **Multiplayer Capabilities**: Online multiplayer support with matchmaking options for competitive play.

## Conclusion
Join us in experiencing the tense atmosphere of the Cold War, where every decision counts and every move could change the fate of nations!
